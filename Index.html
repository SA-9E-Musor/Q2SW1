Stella The fairy of the Shining Sun, known for her fashion style and bright personality. She has the ability to create and control light.

Aisha The fairy of Waves, she's athletic, energetic, and always ready for action. She can control water and fluid movements.
Flora The fairy of Nature, she is gentle, caring, and the peacekeeper of the group. She can manipulate plants and flowers.
Tecna The fairy of Technology, she is logical, analytical, and rational. She has the power to control all forms of technology and digital energy.
